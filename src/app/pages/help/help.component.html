Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Tovrika POS?

Start by creating your account, then set up your Company Profile with business details and BIR information. Next, create your first store in the Stores section, add products to your inventory, and assign user roles. Finally, access the POS system to start processing transactions. You'll get a 14-day free trial to explore all features.

How do I add products to my inventory?

Navigate to the Products section from your dashboard, click "Add Product", and fill in the required details including product name, SKU (optional), barcode (optional), selling price, stock quantity, category, and unit type. You can assign products to specific stores and configure VAT settings (inclusive/exclusive/exempt).

What user roles are available?

Tovrika has three main roles:
‚Ä¢ Business Owner (Creator): Full access to all features
‚Ä¢ Store Manager: Manage stores, inventory, products, and access POS
‚Ä¢ Cashier: POS access only for processing transactions

You can assign users to specific stores based on your subscription plan.

Can I use Tovrika offline?

Yes! Tovrika works offline using secure temporary local storage. Your POS data will automatically sync to the cloud when you reconnect to the internet. You can process orders with custom invoice numbers during offline mode. Note: Registration and password reset require internet connection.

How do I set up BIR compliance for receipts?

In your Store settings, configure your:
‚Ä¢ BIR Permit Number
‚Ä¢ MIN Number
‚Ä¢ Serial Number ranges (From/To)
‚Ä¢ Invoice Type

The system generates BIR-compliant receipts with proper VAT calculations, business information, and payment method indicators. Accreditation is pending.

Can I manage multiple store locations?

Yes! Tovrika supports multi-store management based on your subscription:
‚Ä¢ Standard: 1 store (+‚Ç±500 per additional store)
‚Ä¢ Premium: 1 store (+‚Ç±500 per additional store)
‚Ä¢ Enterprise: Up to 10 branches

Each store can have its own products, managers, cashiers, and BIR settings.

How do I apply discounts in transactions?

The system supports product-level discounts, cart-level discounts, and PWD/Senior Citizen discounts. The default discount type is percentage-based. You can configure discount amounts in the cart during checkout.

How do I view sales reports and analytics?

Navigate to the Sales section from your dashboard to access sales analytics. You can filter by date range, select specific stores, view order details with payment methods, and analyze transaction history. The system provides order status tracking (pending, completed, cancelled).

How do I print receipts?

Tovrika supports multiple printing methods with automatic priority detection:
1. USB/Cable thermal printers (highest priority, requires Web Serial API)
2. Bluetooth thermal printers (mobile/desktop)
3. Browser print fallback (any printer)

The system will automatically detect and use the best available option. You'll see a receipt preview before printing.

How do I upgrade my subscription?

Navigate to the Subscription section, choose your desired plan (Standard, Premium, or Enterprise), select the duration (1, 3, 6, or 12 months), apply any promo codes, and submit your upgrade request with payment details. Your request will be reviewed by Tovrika admin for approval.

What happens when my subscription expires?

Your access will be limited based on your plan. The 14-day free trial allows you to test all features. After the trial, you'll need to subscribe to a paid plan (Standard, Premium, or Enterprise) to maintain access to features, additional stores, and extended data retention periods.
