Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Tovrika POS?

Start by creating your account, then set up your Company Profile with business details and BIR information. Next, create your first store in the Stores section, add products to your inventory, and assign user roles. Finally, access the POS system to start processing transactions.

How do I add products to my inventory?

Navigate to the Products section from your dashboard, click "Add Product", and fill in the required details including product name, SKU, barcode, selling price, stock quantity, and category. You can assign products to specific stores and configure VAT settings.

What user roles are available?

Tovrika has three main roles: Creator (full access to all features), Store Manager (manage stores, inventory, and POS), and Cashier (POS access only). You can also create custom roles with specific permissions in the Access section.

Can I use Tovrika offline?

Yes! Tovrika works completely offline using IndexedDB storage. All transactions and data changes are stored locally and will automatically sync when your internet connection is restored. You can process orders with custom invoice numbers during offline mode.

How do I set up BIR compliance for receipts?

In your Store settings, configure your BIR Permit Number, MIN Number, Serial Numbers, and Invoice Type. The system automatically generates BIR-compliant receipts with proper VAT calculations, customer information, and payment method indicators.

Can I manage multiple store locations?

Absolutely! Tovrika supports multi-store management. Create multiple stores under your company, assign store-specific inventory and user permissions, and manage everything from a single dashboard. Each store can have its own products, managers, and BIR settings.

How do I apply discounts for PWD and Senior Citizens?

In the POS system, click the "Discount" button and select PWD or Senior Citizen discount. Enter the exemption ID if required. The system will automatically calculate the proper discount percentage and generate compliant receipts with the discount information.

How do I view sales reports and analytics?

Navigate to the Sales section from your dashboard to access comprehensive sales analytics. You can filter reports by date range, select specific stores, view order details, and analyze sales performance. The system provides real-time insights into your business metrics.

How do I manage orders and transactions?

In the POS system, use the "Orders" tab to view recent orders, search for specific transactions, and manage order statuses. You can process returns, refunds, and cancellations directly from the order management interface.
