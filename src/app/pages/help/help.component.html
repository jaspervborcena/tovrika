Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add products to my inventory?

Navigate to the Products section from your dashboard, click "Add Product", and fill in the required details including name, price, SKU, and category. You can also bulk import products using our CSV template.

Can I use Tovrika offline?

Yes! Tovrika works completely offline. All transactions and data changes are stored locally and will automatically sync when your internet connection is restored.

How do I process returns and refunds?

From the POS screen, use the "Return" function to scan or select returned items. The system will guide you through the refund process and update inventory automatically.

Can I manage multiple store locations?

Absolutely! Tovrika supports multi-store management. You can add multiple locations, manage separate inventories, and view consolidated or location-specific reports.

How do I generate sales reports?

Go to the Reports section in your dashboard. You can generate various types of reports including daily sales, inventory levels, top-selling products, and custom date ranges.
